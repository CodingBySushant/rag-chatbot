ACME STORE - TECHNICAL TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE
============================================

WEBSITE & APP ISSUES
---------------------

Problem: Cannot log in to my account
Solutions:
1. Check if Caps Lock is ON — passwords are case-sensitive
2. Try "Forgot Password" to reset your password
3. If you signed up with Google/Facebook, use that login option instead
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again
5. Try a different browser or incognito/private mode
6. Disable browser extensions that might block scripts
7. Check if your account is blocked — too many failed attempts lock account for 30 minutes
8. Contact support if none of the above works — provide your registered email

Problem: App is crashing or not loading
Solutions:
1. Force close the app and reopen
2. Check your internet connection
3. Update the app to the latest version from Play Store / App Store
4. Clear app cache: Settings > Apps > ACME Store > Clear Cache
5. Restart your phone
6. Uninstall and reinstall the app (your data is saved to the cloud)
7. Check if there is a known outage at status.acmestore.com

Problem: OTP not received
Solutions:
1. Wait 60 seconds before requesting a new OTP
2. Check if your mobile number is correct
3. Check SMS spam/blocked messages folder
4. Ensure you have mobile network (not just WiFi for SMS)
5. Try the "Call me instead" option for OTP via call
6. If issue persists for more than 10 minutes, contact support

Problem: Payment page not loading
Solutions:
1. Disable VPN if active
2. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
3. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different payment method
6. Check if your bank's website is accessible (may be a bank-side issue)

ORDER RELATED ISSUES
--------------------

Problem: My order shows delivered but I haven't received it
Steps to resolve:
1. Check with family members or neighbors — someone may have accepted on your behalf
2. Check with building security or reception
3. Check the delivery photo if available in Order Tracking
4. Wait 24 hours — sometimes delivery status updates before actual delivery
5. If still not received after 24 hours, raise a complaint in My Orders > Report an Issue
6. We will investigate with the courier and resolve within 48 hours
7. If confirmed undelivered, a replacement or full refund will be issued

Problem: Order is stuck "Out for Delivery" for more than 2 days
Steps:
1. Click "Track Order" to see real-time location
2. Call the delivery partner using the number in Order Tracking
3. If unreachable, raise a complaint through the app/website
4. We will contact the courier partner and resolve within 24 hours

Problem: Item missing from my order
Steps:
1. Check if items were shipped in separate packages (common for multi-item orders)
2. Check if all packages have been delivered (track each separately)
3. If a package is missing, raise a complaint within 48 hours with your order ID
4. Provide photos of what you received and the outer packaging
5. Missing item will be reshipped or refunded within 3-5 business days

PAYMENT ISSUES
--------------

Problem: Money deducted but order not placed
This is a common issue with payment gateways. Here is what happens:
- The amount is held by your bank/payment provider
- If the order wasn't created, the hold is auto-released within 5-7 business days
- You do NOT need to contact your bank — it will resolve automatically
- Check your email for any order confirmation
- If you don't receive a refund within 7 business days, contact us with your transaction ID

Problem: Coupon code not working
Common reasons:
1. Coupon has expired — check the validity date
2. Minimum order value not met — check coupon terms
3. Product category not eligible — some coupons apply to specific categories
4. Already used — most coupons are one-time use per account
5. Not applicable on sale items — check if coupon excludes discounted products
6. Incorrect entry — check for spaces or typos, codes are case-insensitive

Problem: EMI option not showing at checkout
Reasons this may happen:
1. Your card may not be eligible — only select bank cards support no-cost EMI
2. Order value below ₹3000 — minimum threshold for EMI
3. Product may be excluded from EMI offers
4. Bank server issue — try again after some time
Contact your bank to confirm if your card supports EMI purchases.

RETURNS & REFUND ISSUES
------------------------

Problem: Return request rejected
Possible reasons:
1. Return window expired (30 days for most products, 7 days for electronics)
2. Product shows signs of use or damage not present at delivery
3. Original packaging or tags missing
4. Product category not eligible for returns (opened personal care, customized items)
If you believe the rejection was incorrect, reply to your return rejection email with supporting photos to appeal.

Problem: Refund not received after 10 days
Steps:
1. Check your refund status in My Orders > Return/Refund Status
2. Confirm the refund was approved and processed date
3. Check the refund mode — it goes back to original payment method
4. For credit/debit cards, check with your bank using the Refund Reference ID we provide
5. For UPI, check all linked UPI IDs in your UPI app
6. Contact support with your Return ID and we will investigate with payment gateway

ACCOUNT & SECURITY ISSUES
--------------------------

Problem: Suspicious activity or unauthorized orders
Immediate steps:
1. Change your password immediately via Forgot Password
2. Go to Account Settings > Active Sessions and log out all other devices
3. Check if your registered email or phone was changed — report to support immediately
4. Review recent orders and raise dispute for any unauthorized purchases
5. Contact support at security@acmestore.com for urgent cases
6. We recommend enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) in Account Settings > Security

Problem: Cannot update phone number — OTP going to old number I no longer have access to
Resolution process:
1. Email support@acmestore.com from your registered email address
2. Subject: "Mobile Number Update - Account Verification Required"
3. Provide: Full name, email, old mobile number, new mobile number, last order ID
4. Our team will verify your identity and update manually within 24-48 hours
5. You may be asked to submit a government ID for verification
