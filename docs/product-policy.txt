ACME STORE - PRODUCT WARRANTY & SUPPORT GUIDE
=============================================

ELECTRONICS WARRANTY POLICY
----------------------------

All electronics purchased from ACME Store come with the following warranty coverage:

1. SMARTPHONES & TABLETS
   - Manufacturer warranty: 12 months from date of purchase
   - Screen damage: Not covered under standard warranty (accidental damage protection available at ₹499/year)
   - Battery replacement: Covered for 6 months if capacity drops below 80%
   - Software issues: Covered for lifetime via manufacturer support
   - Water damage: Not covered (unless device is rated IP67/IP68)

2. LAPTOPS & COMPUTERS
   - Manufacturer warranty: 12 months (extended 2-year warranty available)
   - Carries-in service: Customer must bring device to authorized service center
   - On-site service: Available for purchases above ₹50,000
   - Data loss: Not covered — always backup your data
   - Battery warranty: 6 months

3. HOME APPLIANCES
   - Warranty period: 12 months standard, up to 5 years on compressors (refrigerators/ACs)
   - Free installation: Available for washing machines, ACs, and refrigerators in metro cities
   - Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC): Available after warranty period
   - Spare parts availability: Guaranteed for 7 years from purchase date

4. AUDIO & ACCESSORIES
   - Earphones / Headphones: 6 months warranty
   - Speakers: 12 months warranty
   - Cables and adapters: 3 months warranty
   - Physical damage and water damage void the warranty

HOW TO CLAIM WARRANTY
----------------------

Step 1: Check if your issue is covered under warranty (see exclusions below)
Step 2: Locate your invoice (emailed at time of purchase, also available in My Orders)
Step 3: Call brand service center or visit acmestore.com/warranty-claim
Step 4: Enter your order ID and describe the issue
Step 5: Our team will arrange pickup (for eligible products) or guide you to nearest service center
Step 6: Repair or replacement completed within 7-15 working days

WARRANTY EXCLUSIONS
-------------------

The following are NOT covered under warranty:
- Physical damage (cracks, dents, broken ports)
- Liquid/water damage (unless IP rated)
- Damage due to incorrect voltage or power surges
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Normal wear and tear (scratches, faded color)
- Consumables (bulbs, filters, belts)
- Damage during transit after delivery (report within 48 hours)
- Loss or theft

PRODUCT CATEGORIES & CARE TIPS
--------------------------------

CLOTHING & FOOTWEAR:
- Refer to the care label on each product for washing instructions
- Colors may bleed during first wash — wash dark colors separately
- Do not tumble dry unless specified
- Store footwear in the provided dust bag

SKINCARE & BEAUTY:
- Check expiry date before use
- Patch test recommended for sensitive skin
- Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
- Products cannot be returned once opened (unless defective)

FOOD & GROCERY:
- Best before date is printed on all food products
- Cold chain products are delivered in insulated packaging
- If seal is broken or product is damaged on arrival, do not consume — contact support immediately

FURNITURE & HOME DECOR:
- Self-assembly products come with instruction manual
- Free assembly service available in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Pune (charges: ₹299-999 depending on product)
- Mattresses have a 100-night trial — return if not satisfied
- Fabric furniture: professional cleaning recommended
